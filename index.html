About Me

I'm a Software Quality Analyst for Kenco Group, a third-party logistics and warehousing provider. More recently, I'm also learning and helping a variety of our departments implement Robotic Process Automation. RPA can be used across a variety of applications to automate repetitive business processes, whether financial, transportation, warehousing, or software testing-related.

"So," one may ask, "How is RPA related to Full-Stack Web Development?" As it turns out, many of the applications with which we interact in day-to-day business are browser-based! Beyond RPA, for me, discovering how web-based applications work (and making my own!) is food for my insatiable curiosity and also nurishment for my creative soul.
